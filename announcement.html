Important Announcement

16th March 2019

Bitcratic.com had deployed new Smart Contract on 16th March 19, users who have deposited ETH/Tokens before 16th March 19 can withdraw ETH/Tokens from old Smart contract from https://www.myetherwallet.com/

  • Old Bitcratic Smart contract - 0x232ba9f3b3643ab28d28ed7ee18600708d60e5fe
  • The Bitcratic contract ABI, found on the etherscan page of the contract here
  • https://etherscan.io/address/0x232ba9f3b3643ab28d28ed7ee18600708d60e5fe#code
  • The address of the token contract. Found on etherscan or on Bitcratic.com itself by clicking the token name in the token dropdown next to help. (etherscan example)(use 0 as the address for ETH)
  • Your own wallet address and private key
Interacting with the contract
  • Go to myetherwallet and click the contracts tab.
  • Double check to make sure it is the real site and not a phishing copy.
  • Paste the contract address and abi and click access
  • You can now use the 'select a function' dropdown
Gettting your balance in wei units
  • Select 'balanceOf' and enter the token address and your wallet address and click
  • This gives you how much you have in Bitcratic old Smart Contract, in wei. (1 ETH = 1000000000000000000 wei) Copy this number somewhere.
Withdrawing tokens to your wallet
  • Select 'withdrawToken' and enter the token address and the amount you just copied
  • unlock your wallet with your private key or Meta Mask, click write and accept the transaction.
  • The ETH value sent in the transaction popup should be 0, gas limit is filled automatically
Withdrawing ETH to your wallet
  • Select 'withdraw' and enter the amount you just copied.
  • Unlock your wallet with your private key Or Meta Mask, click write and accept the transaction
  • The ETH value sent in the transaction popup should be 0, gas limit is filled automatically
